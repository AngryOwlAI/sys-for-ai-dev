# Self-hosting policy

A trusted release may propose or implement a successor only in a separate
candidate workspace. Independent verification, a rollback release, and human
promotion are mandatory. A candidate cannot approve its purpose, permissions,
evaluation threshold, safety waiver, production status, or release promotion.
