# Target packages

A generated target has explicit governance, requirements, architecture,
runtime, skills, contracts, tests, operations, and evidence layers. The
`target-system.yaml` manifest identifies it as derivative and sets
`production_ready: false`.

The target package may represent one agent, a workflow, a multi-agent system,
an agentic application, a reusable framework, or an organizational platform.
Its accountable owner must approve requirements, validate intended use, and
authorize operation independently of Sys4AI product development.

Generated target fixtures belong outside the product source tree. They prove
only that the declared generation and validation flow executed under the
recorded conditions.
