# Roles and skills

Roles identify responsibility and bounded decision rights. Skills identify
reusable methods. A role may use several skills, and a skill never grants role
authority.

The canonical product role types are in
`contracts/catalogs/role-types.yaml`. The canonical product skill assets are in
`assets/skills/catalog.yaml`. The detailed guides explain the relationship but
do not replace those catalogs.

Development skills may be reviewed and generalized into product assets.
Promotion is explicit; no host binding or development-state assumption is
copied automatically.
