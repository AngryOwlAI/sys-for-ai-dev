# Project role and skill example

Consider a fictional shelter-coordination assistant called HarborLight. The
target owner retains authority over service purpose, case decisions, affected
people, data, integrations, deployment, and retirement. Sys4AI supplies methods
and contracts but does not become HarborLight's operator.

A bounded team might use:

- `user_wants_elicitor` with `system-definition-interview` to gather scenarios;
- `requirements_manager` with `requirements-discovery-governor` to promote
  accepted requirements;
- `system_architect` with `interface-and-integration-discovery` to define data
  flows and owners;
- `security_safety_privacy_compliance_reviewer` with
  `threat-model-and-permission-scope` to identify protection obligations;
- `bounded_execution_planner` with `prd-to-implementation-plan` to define
  reversible work;
- `verification_engineer` with `verification-validation-planner` and
  `evaluation-harness-designer` to test explicit claims;
- `runtime_maintenance_planner` with `operations-and-maintenance-planner` to
  establish monitoring, incidents, updates, improvement, and retirement.

This association does not authorize any real shelter workflow. Domain experts,
affected-party review, privacy and safety evidence, operational ownership, and
human promotion remain required.
