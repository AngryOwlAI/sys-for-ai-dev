# Skills guide

Product skills are versioned, host-neutral methods. The product catalog lists
only reviewed generalized assets. A host binding, local tool, or development
workflow does not become a product skill merely because it has a similar name.

A role-to-skill association says that the method may support the role; it does
not grant decision rights or execution permission. Every skill remains bounded
by source authority, its declared outcomes, the current target layer, the host,
and the transaction permission envelope.

The baseline catalog includes discovery, requirements, architecture,
governance, assurance, traceability, security, operations, documentation,
knowledge, diagramming, and implementation-planning methods. Host-specific
session accounting and context-threshold variants remain outside the portable
catalog.

See `assets/skills/catalog.yaml` and
`contracts/catalogs/skill-types.yaml`.
